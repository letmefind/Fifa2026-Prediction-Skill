How To Read

Model Notes

  • Step 1: Click Refresh Latest Data to fetch new scores and rebuild the model.
  • One match: Use Predict Match — no simulation needed.
  • Full group: Use Predict Groups — shows played scores + future games + table.
  • Tournament winner: Use Run Simulation only when you need World Cup or knockout odds.
  • Expected goals estimate scoring volume, not an exact final score.
  • Betting EV is not a guarantee. Always check injuries, lineups, and market liquidity.

Betting Help

How To Find Model Probability

1. Predict the match

Choose two teams and click Predict Match. The three result cards are the model probabilities.

2. Pick your market

For Brazil win, use Brazil win probability. For draw, use draw probability. For France win, use France win probability.

3. Convert to decimal

Use decimal form in EV: 35.6% = 0.356. The match cards include Use for EV buttons.

4. Add market odds

For Polymarket, approximate decimal odds as 1 / Yes price. A 0.42 price becomes 2.38 odds.

Odds Help

What Are Decimal Odds?

Decimal odds = payout multiplier

Odds 2.20 means a winning $1 stake returns $2.20 total: $1.20 profit plus your $1 stake.

Implied probability

Market probability is 1 / decimal odds. Odds 2.20 implies 45.5%.

Polymarket conversion

A Yes price of 0.42 is roughly 42%. Decimal odds are 1 / 0.42 = 2.38.

American odds conversion

+150 becomes 2.50. -150 becomes 1.67. Use the converted decimal odds in the EV calculator.
