  • Tournament odds stabilize as simulation runs increase.
  • Betting EV is not a guarantee. Always check injuries, lineups, and market liquidity.

Betting Help

How To Find Model Probability

1. Predict the match

Choose two teams and click Predict Match. The three result cards are the model probabilities.

2. Pick your market

For Brazil win, use Brazil win probability. For draw, use draw probability. For France win, use France win probability.

3. Convert to decimal

Use decimal form in EV: 35.6% = 0.356. The match cards include Use for EV buttons.

4. Add market odds

For Polymarket, approximate decimal odds as 1 / Yes price. A 0.42 price becomes 2.38 odds.
